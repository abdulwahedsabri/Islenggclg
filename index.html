Artificial Intelligence & Data Science

Learn to teach machines how to think and make decisions! Dive into the fascinating world of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science in our college course. Discover how computers learn patterns from data and make predictions, and explore the algorithms that power everything from virtual assistants to self-driving cars. Join us to unlock the secrets of AI and Data Science!

Computer Science and Engineering

Embark on a journey into the heart of technology! Enroll in our college Computer Science and Engineering course and explore the endless possibilities of coding, software development, and hardware design. Dive into the world of algorithms, data structures, and computer architecture. Whether you dream of creating the next big app or revolutionizing artificial intelligence, this course will equip you with the skills to shape the digital future. Join us and unleash your potential in the dynamic field of Computer Science and Engineering!

Civil Engineering

Build the future with us! Enroll in our college Civil Engineering course and learn how to design, construct, and maintain the infrastructure that shapes our world. From bridges to buildings, roads to railways, delve into the principles of structural engineering, transportation, and environmental sustainability. Join us and become a master builder of tomorrow!

Electronics and communication Engineering

Step into the world of connectivity and innovation! Join our college Electronics and Communication Engineering course to explore the exciting realm of circuits, signals, and communication technologies. Learn how to design and optimize electronic devices, from smartphones to satellite systems. Dive into topics like digital communication, wireless networks, and semiconductor technology. Join us and become a pioneer in shaping the future of communication!

Information technology

Unlock the power of technology with us! Enroll in our college Information Technology course and delve into the dynamic world of computers, networks, and software systems. Learn to harness the latest tools and techniques in programming, cybersecurity, and database management. Whether you aspire to develop cutting-edge applications or safeguard digital infrastructure, this course will equip you with the skills to thrive in the fast-paced IT industry. Join us and embark on a journey to shape the digital landscape!.

Mechanical engineering

Master the mechanics of innovation! Enroll in our college Mechanical Engineering course and delve into the fascinating world of machines, materials, and motion. From designing sleek automobiles to crafting efficient power plants, explore the principles of mechanics, thermodynamics, and manufacturing. Whether you dream of inventing groundbreaking technologies or solving complex engineering challenges, this course will empower you to engineer the future. Join us and ignite your passion for innovation in Mechanical Engineering!.

About College

ISL Engineering College


Technical institute Established 2008

ISL Engineering College is affiliated to Osmania University, and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education Estd: 2008, it is a private engineering college located in Hyderabad, Telangana. The college is run and maintained by the Ahmed Memorial Educational society. The college offers bachelor of engineering (B.E) in six courses and two masters Courses, namely Artificial Intelligence & Data Science, Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and communication Engineering, Information technology & Mechanical engineering
